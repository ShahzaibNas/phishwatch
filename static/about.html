About PhishWatch

Enterprise-grade detection, without the enterprise price.

Protecting your brand from impersonation shouldn't require a six-figure security budget. PhishWatch turns the same public infrastructure that secures the web into an early-warning system any team can use.

Our mission

Close the gap between free tools and enterprise platforms

Every day, attackers register thousands of lookalike domains — subtle misspellings, swapped characters, and non-Latin homographs designed to pass as the real thing. They clone login pages, harvest credentials, and disappear within hours.

Enterprise brand-protection platforms detect these attacks well, but they're priced for large security teams with large budgets. Free tools exist, but they stop at generating a list of possibilities — leaving you to figure out which ones are actually dangerous. PhishWatch sits in the middle: the detection depth of the expensive tools, delivered simply and affordably.

How we detect

Built on Certificate Transparency

Certificate Transparency logs are public, append-only records of every SSL/TLS certificate issued on the web. Because nearly every phishing site needs a certificate to look legitimate, these logs act as a near-real-time feed of new domains going live — and that's exactly what we watch.
